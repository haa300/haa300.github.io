Cultivating HAA150: Human-Centric Atomic Action Dataset with Curated Video

We contribute HAA150,a manually annotated human-centric atomic action dataset for action recognition on 150 classes with over 240k labeled frames. Unlike existing atomic action datasets some of which were collected in the wild, HAA150 has been carefully curated to capture movement of human figures with less spatio-temporal noises to greatly enhance the training of deep neural networks. The advantages of HAA150 include: 1) human-centric actions with a high average of 75.1% detectable joints for the relevant human poses; 2) high sampling frequency which effectively ameliorates the adverse effect of temporal noises; 3) fine-grained atomic action classes. Our extensive experiments have validated that 018 with its clean and fine-grained nature, HAA150 significantly improves action recognition tasks even only adopting baseline architecture under different settings. We detail the HAA150 dataset statistics and collection methodology, and compare quantitatively with existing action recognition datasets.
